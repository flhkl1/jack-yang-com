Who I am

I'm Jack Yang (杨天禧). I'm a senior at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science, Mathematics, and German, and student researcher at the UCSF NeuroAI Lab.

What I work on

My research currently focuses on engineering large language models (LLMs) for dementia diagnosis.

That is to say, I train LLMs to ingest patient interviews, audio recordings, and biomarker data and output high-confidence diagnosis reports of dementia, which include a set of decision rules and chains of reasoning as to how the model arrived at its conclusion.

How I got here

Previously, I was an SDE at AWS Bedrock on Infra and Ops for AWS' Anthropic partnership. I also worked on software at LiveX.ai and as an investment analyst with a focus on InfraAI investment theses at Ocean Arete Fund in Hong Kong.
